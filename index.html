It's all about versatility

Hi, I'm Terry, web developer, digital marketer, ex database developer and writer. I used to work with Visual Basic, ASP.net and C#. Indeed, I have 3 MCAD certifications for .net 2.0 with C#. After a period away from coding and exploring other things (becoming a published author of literary fiction along the way), I've realised that coding is the one thing I love above all else.

I have a passion for learning. I'm always doing some kind of course, and in my spare time I read books and watch documentaries that challenge the establishment. I've ghost-written one such book, about a story that made global headlines.

I hope you enjoy viewing my portfolio. Feel free to email me on terencemartin1@sky.com with your questions.
